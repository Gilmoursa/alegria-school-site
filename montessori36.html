Montessori Beginnings 3 - 6



Montessori 3 - 6

These parent education classes are especially created for children from birth to two years of age and are a wonderful way to spend time with your child in a Montessori environment. Our Montessori trained facilitator will cover topics such as Montessori environments, independence, toilet teaching, positive discipline, observation, weaning, language development, and more! Classes will be held twice per week and will include an observation period, a parent discussion period, and a Music Together class designed for babies and toddlers. Please contact us for pricing and more information.
