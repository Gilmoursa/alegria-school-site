Montessori Beginnings 0 - 3

"…children are human beings to whom respect is due, superior to us by reason of their innocence and of the greater possibilities of their future…Let us treat them…with all the kindness which we would wish to help to develop in them."
– Maria Montessori, Dr. Montessori’s Own Handbook.


Parent & Child

These parent education classes are especially created for children from birth to two years of age and are a wonderful way to spend time with your child in a Montessori environment. Our Montessori trained facilitator will cover topics such as Montessori environments, independence, toilet teaching, positive discipline, observation, weaning, language development, and more! Classes will be held twice per week and will include an observation period, a parent discussion period, and a Music Together class designed for babies and toddlers. Please contact us for pricing and more information.

One-Year Olds

A child at twelve months is just beginning to experience the world. In the next year, he or she will probably begin to walk and run, talk in simple sentences, feed him or herself, and many other milestones. The ones classroom at Alegria Montessori has been designed by our Montessori trained teachers to present your young toddler with a safe, engaging, and loving space in which to explore, gain confidence, and practice independence. We offer a language rich environment that respects the autonomy and individuality of each child in our care. Sensory experimentation and movement are encouraged, with a toddler friendly outdoor playground, covered porch, and garden.

Two-Year Olds

Between the ages of 24 months and 3 years of age, a child develops explosively – body control (running, jumping, and dancing), fine motor control (tweezing, spooning, pouring, and grasping), language development (speaking in sentences and paragraphs, telling and listening to stories, and singing), and socially and emotionally (beginning to make friends and learn social graces). Your two year old is busy!

Our Montessori trained teachers offer a sensory and language rich environment that paves the way for future successes. We offer many practical life skills to a child of this age, which hones motor control and independence. At Alegria Montessori School, we have prepared a safe, friendly, and ordered classroom for your child to practice these skills and build concentration, independence, and confidence.

Tanya Rotter
Site Director / Toddler Teacher: One-Year Olds

Tanya Rotter taught in a traditional preschool in Kentucky for 8 years before moving to Florida, working with children from 1 year to 12 years of age. She explored other career options in retail management before returning to her real love – teaching! Tanya has worked at Alegria for 6 years. Tanya is the lead teacher and site director at our Alegria Lakewood campus. She will also coordinate the Parent/Infant Education classes. Tanya attended Eckerd College, has her Child Development Associate, and has finally done her 0 – 3 Montessori training with Palm Harbor Teacher Education Center!

Rachel Manzo
Parent/Child Class and Toddler Teacher: One-Year Olds

After babysitting and nannying for many years, Rachel fell in love with Montessori when she was introduced to Montessori Parent/Child classes. She joined Alegria in 2012.
